Route H Summer Camp

Experiential learning for Japanese Students

Yale-NUS College hosts 14 Japanese high school students for one week, from August 1, 2015 to August 10, 2015. In this camp, participants will be exposed to a liberal arts curriculum in the unique setting of multicultural Singapore.
